Privacy

Your data stays with you

At BreakOff, we believe your privacy is paramount. That's why we've designed our app with a simple privacy principle:

All your data is stored directly on your device.

We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on our servers. Your break times, app preferences, and usage patterns remain completely private and under your control.

This means your information never leaves your device, and we have no access to your data whatsoever.

Privacy Policy for BreakOff: Digital Wellness

Last updated: February 23, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to BreakOff: Digital Wellness. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

2. Data Collection

We practice data minimization. The only data the app may ask for is:

Name (Optional): Used to address you personally within the app. Stored only on your device.

Age (Optional): Used to tailor the app experience to your life stage. Stored only on your device.

Providing this information is strictly voluntary. You may skip these steps during onboarding and still enjoy the full functionality of the BreakOff app. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

3. How We Use Your Data

Any data you choose to provide is used solely for in-app personalization.

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

We do not use your data for advertising tracking purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored locally on your device using your operating system's built-in security protections. We do not operate servers that store your personal information. Since your data never leaves your device, there is no risk of a server-side data breach.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Since all data is stored on your device, you have full control over it at all times.

Delete via App: You can clear all app data directly within the app's Settings menu.

Delete via Uninstall: Removing the BreakOff app from your device deletes all associated data.

If you have any questions about data deletion, you may contact us at support@breakoff.io.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@breakoff.io
